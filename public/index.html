Our Story

A Legacy of Excellence

Where Dreams Meet Reality

The L'Arche De La Vie is an exceptional hotel and event venue situated in the heart of Gauteng, South Africa. Located at the corner of Smith and Small Street, Benoni, this establishment offers a unique and captivating experience for business trips or tourists.

With its charming and elegant atmosphere, L'Arche De La Vie stands out among other accommodations in the area. Here, you will be welcomed by a dedicated and friendly team who will make your stay as comfortable and enjoyable as possible.

The owners' commitment to excellence shines through every aspect of their venue, making it a standout choice for any visitor. Whether you are on a business trip or exploring the city as a tourist, L'Arche De La Vie is an ideal base to relax and unwind.
